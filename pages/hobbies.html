// Gaming Stats

The numbers

I've put real time into these games. It's not just entertainment—every one teaches me something about design, engagement, or community.

League of Legends

3000+ hours over 8+ years. I understand MOBAs at a deep level—champion balance, meta shifts, esports ecosystem. This game taught me how live-ops can keep a game relevant for a decade.

3000+ Hours 8+ Years

Teamfight Tactics

1000+ hours. Hit Master rank. Auto-battlers are fascinating from a design perspective—so much complexity with minimal inputs. Taught me about economy systems and adaptive strategy.

1000+ Hours Master Rank

Arknights

1000+ hours. Tower defense gacha with incredible worldbuilding. I study how they do live-ops, limited banners, and narrative integration. The art direction is top-tier.

1000+ Hours Tower Defense

Fate/Grand Order

Classic gacha RPG. Interesting case study in IP leverage and how narrative quality can compensate for dated gameplay. The Type-Moon universe keeps people engaged for years.
